Himachal

Description

Day 01: Delhi - Shimla (350Kms / 08 – 09 Hrs )

Pick up from Ashram Metro Station Delhi at 09 AM morning Will Pick Up & After Proceed To Shimla. Evening Shimla Check in To The Hotel. Rest of The Day Is At Leisure, Later Back To Hotel. Overnight Stay at A Hotel In Shimla.

Day 02: Shimla –Local & Kufri Sightseeing - Shimla (24 Kms.)

After Breakfast Visit To Half Day City Tour Visiting Indian Institute Of Advanced Studies, Afternoon Visit To Excursion Tour Of Kufri Sightseeing: Green Valley, WildFlower Hall, Kufri, Indira Tourist Park, Mini Zoo & Fagu Valley. Back to Hotel. Dinner & Overnight Stay at Hotel.

Day 03: Shimla – En-route Kullu - Manali (280 Kms / 7 Hrs)

After Breakfast Check Out from Shimla Hotel Visit to Enroute Kullu Sightseeing Like: Beas River, Vaishno Temple & Famous Kullu Shawl Industry. Drive to Manali. Check in The Hotel, Rest of The Day Is At Leisure, Dinner & Overnight Stay At Hotel At Manali.

Day 04: Manali ( Local Sightseeing & Jogini Waterfall Tracking Point )

After Breakfasts & Some Time Enjoy The City Tour Visiting To Some Of The Prominent Spots And Attractions Including Also Called, Club House, Tibetan Monastery And Vashisth Village Van Vihar Mall Road . And Visit to Jogini Waterfall Tracking Point After Enjoying Exciting Tour of Manali City Return Back by I’ll Be at The Hotel in Manali.

Day 05: Manali - Full Day Trip to Solang Valley (Anjani Mahadev Tracking Point)

Enjoy the Morning Breakfast at The Hotel and Get Ready for An Excursion to Visit Solang Valley: A Beautiful Picnic Spot. Nice Meadows Surrounded by Tree of Silver Oak and Spruce. Famous or International Skiing Slopes. Beautiful Panorama of Snow, Enjoy Horse Riding and Paragliding. Return Back to The Hotel on Time for Dinner and Overnight Stay.

Day 06: Manali – Delhi (12 - 14 Hrs/ 550 Kms)

Morning After Breakfast Ch./Out from Manali Hotel Proceeds to Delhi. Evening Drop At, Delhi Railway Station, Airport with Onward Journey.
