Inclusion and Exclusion criteria

  • Entry ticket to Statue of Unity is included

Cancellation Policy

  • 100% Cancellation Charges, if cancelled within 7 days from the check-in date
  • 50% Cancellation charges, if cancelled within 10 days from the check-in date
  • 25% Cancellation charges, if cancelled within 15 days from the check-in date
  • 15% Cancellation charges, if cancelled in more than 15 days from the check-in date
  • Date amendment charges are applicable as per cancellation Policy above

Note

  • Statue of Unity will remain closed every Monday.
  • For the guests checking-in the resort on Sunday, the same-day Statue of Unity visit will be arranged. (Statue of Unity visit time : 9.00 am to 5.00 pm)
