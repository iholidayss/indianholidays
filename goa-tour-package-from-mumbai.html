Goa Tour Package

Description

Day : 1 Mumbai (Arrival)

After arriving at the Mumbai airport, check in to the hotel/resort for a pre-booked stay. Overnight stay at hotel

Day : 2 Mumbai to Goa

Have a delightful breakfast and you’ll be transferred to the Mumbai airport to board your flight for the journey back to Goa. After arriving at the Dabolim Airport in Goa, check in to the hotel/resort for a pre-booked stay. Overnight stay at the hotel in Goa.

Day : 3 Day at Goa

The next two days are allocated to explore the beaches of Goa and all other places they have to offer. Enjoy sunbathing, gorge on the scrumptious seafood, try various water sports, take a leisurely stroll on the beach, and enjoy electric beach parties. Visit Dona Paula Seashore, Miramar Beachfront, Anjuna Beach, Baga Beach, Chapora Seaside, Vagator Seaside and Benaulim Seaside.

Day : 4 Day at Goa

The next two days are allocated to explore the beaches of Goa and all other places they have to offer. Enjoy sunbathing, gorge on the scrumptious seafood, try various water sports, take a leisurely stroll on the beach, and enjoy electric beach parties. Visit Dona Paula Seashore, Miramar Beachfront, Anjuna Beach, Baga Beach, Chapora Seaside, Vagator Seaside and Benaulim Seaside.

Day : 5 Goa

Enjoy your breakfast in the morning, get out of your hotel and get a glimpse of Goa’s lifestyle. Enjoy cruise ride on the Mandovi River along with a game of volleyball on the beach. Apart from this, there are a variety of water sports which you can enjoy like speed-boating, scuba diving, parasailing and windsurfing. Overnight stay at hotel.

Day : 6 Goa Onwards

At the last day of your awesome journey, we have to say goodbye to you in hope to see you again. Early in morning after having the breakfast check out from hotel and Departure to your destination via railway Station or airport.
