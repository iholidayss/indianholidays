Saurashtra

Description

Saurashtra, also known as Sorath or Kathiawar, is a peninsular region of Gujarat, India, located on the Arabian Sea coast. It covers about a third of Gujarat state, notably 11 districts of Gujarat, including Rajkot District. It was formerly a state of India before it merged with Bombay state.

Day 01: Ahmedabad – Jamnagar - Dwarka (450kms / 09hrs Approx.)

Once you arrive at the airport/station, meet our representative at Ahmedabad and later continue drive to Dwarka. On arrival visit Lakota Lake and lakhota Museum. In the evening visit Bala Hanuman Temple – Known for its continuous chanting of Ramdhun, for which it mentioned in guiness book of world record. Later continue drive to Dwarka. Evening visit attend aarti at Dwarkadhish temple. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 02: Jamnagar – Dwarka (150kms/ 03hrs Approx.)

Today Morning after breakfast proceed to Dwarka. On arrive at Dwarka visit Dwarkadhish Temple. Later drive to visit Nageshwar temple, Shivrajpur beach, Gopi Talav, and Bet Dwarka. While return back to Dwarka enroute visit Rukmani temple. Overnight stay at Dwarka.

Day 03: Dwarka – Porbandar – Somnath (225kms/ 05hrs Approx.)

Today Morning departs to Porbandar. At Porbandar visit Kirti Mandir – Place where Gandhi ji was born. Later drive to somnath. On arrive at Somnath visit Somnath temple, Bhalka tirth and Triveni Ghat. In the evening attend Aarti and Later Watch light and sound show. Overnight stay at Somnath.

Day 04: Somnath – Diu (90km/ 2hrs)

Today morning Proceed to beach town Diu. The day is at leisure to enjoy the wide variety of water sports facilities available at Nagoa Beach. Later visit St. Paul's Church, Diu Fortress, Naida caves – natural rock. Overnight stay at Diu.

Day 05: Diu – Sasangir (100km/2hrs)

Today, proceed to Sasangir. Sasangir, is the last home of the Asiatic Lion. In the afternoon Visit Gir Devaliya Safari, Crocodile breeding farm, Gir Information centre. In the evening watch movie on lion at Gir Information centre. Overnight stay at Sasangir.

Day 06: Sasangir - Ahmedabad (225km/4hrs)

Early morning gets ready for open Jeep Safari in the Gir National Park. Later proceed to Ahmedabad. In the evening drop at Airport or railway station.
